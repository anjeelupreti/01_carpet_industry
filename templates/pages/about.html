Our Legacy

Built on decades of expertise in Tibetan Art and Antiques

Established in 1986

After two decades of experience in the Tibetan Art and Antique field, we established the EVEREST MEMORIAL CARPET INDUSTRY in 1986, bringing our deep understanding of Tibetan culture into carpet craftsmanship.

"Unlike common bazaar goods, EMCI handles the entire carpet manufacturing process under our own roof, ensuring unparalleled quality control and authenticity in every piece we create."
