Our Heritage

Preserving Tibetan Heritage Through Craftsmanship

After two decades of experience in the Tibetan Art and Antique field, we established Everest Carpet Industry in 1986 to continue the ancient tradition of Tibetan carpet weaving.

Unlike common bazaar goods, we handle the entire manufacturing process under our own roof - from raw wool sorting and processing to hand carding, spinning, dyeing, weaving, trimming, washing, packaging and labeling the finished products.
