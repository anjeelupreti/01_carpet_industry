Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about ordering and our products

How long does it take to make a custom carpet?

Production time varies by quality: 60 knots/sq inch - 45 days, 80 knots/sq inch - 60 days, 100 knots/sq inch - 90 days. Additional time may be needed for complex designs.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes, we ship worldwide. We handle all export documentation and work with reliable shipping partners to ensure safe delivery to any destination.

Can I get samples before ordering?

Yes, we provide wool samples and color swatches. For custom designs, we can create small sample swatches before full production begins.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept bank transfers, credit cards (via secure payment gateway), and in some cases, PayPal. Terms vary for wholesale/bulk orders.
